MOHWA only needs his magical fingers to revitalize your mind and enable you to experience an intricate mix of new sensations and nostalgic emotions. His beautiful tremolo will make you reminisce your fondest memories, his powerful flamenco strums will give you heart palpitations and the tone of his guitar will give you a warm fuzzy feeling that intensifies until climax is reached.
